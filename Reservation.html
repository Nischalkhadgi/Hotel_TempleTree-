Reservation Policy

To make a reservation a pre-payment of 25% of the entire stay.

The waiting period for a down payment is 3 days.
After this period the reservation will be canceled.

When making the transfer, please provide the name and
the dates of your stay on the reservation that is made.

The fee for the stay is charged in advance at check-in the guest house,
in case of early cancellation of stay or shorten your stay
(Delay arrival or early departure,failure mode of transport, illness, weather conditions)
- downloaded pledge and charge per stay is not refundable .
